The Abacus

How the beads work

The abacus has three rods: Hundreds, Tens, and Ones. Each rod can hold beads.

Move beads toward the centre (the counting side) of each rod to make them active. Every active bead adds one unit to that rod. To remove a unit, move the bead back.

Click or tap any bead to toggle it. On a keyboard, use Tab to focus a bead and Enter or Space to activate it.

Place Value

What each rod means

Rod Bead Colour Each Bead Equals Example
Hundreds Red 100 3 beads = 300
Tens Blue 10 2 beads = 20
Ones Green 1 4 beads = 4

Worked Example

Building 324 on the abacus

324

Hundreds: 3 active beads = 300

Tens: 2 active beads = 20

Ones: 4 active beads = 4

Total: 300 + 20 + 4 = 324

Answering Questions

Step by step

  1. Read the question

    The question appears at the top of the game screen. Read it carefully before moving any beads.

  2. Build the answer on the abacus

    Click or tap beads to move them toward the counting side. The "Current Number" display updates live as you adjust beads.

  3. Press Submit Answer

    When you are happy with your answer, click the blue "Submit Answer" button in the sidebar.

  4. Use Hint if needed

    If you are stuck, press the "Hint" button. A helpful clue will appear beneath the question.

  5. Reset to start over

    Press "Reset Abacus" to clear all beads back to zero and start building from scratch.

Stars & Rewards

How scoring works

Stars reward accurate answers and completed levels. After finishing all questions in an activity, you receive 1, 2, or 3 stars based on how many you answered correctly.

⭐ 1 Star — You completed the level. Well done!

⭐⭐ 2 Stars — Most answers were correct. Great work!

⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars — All or nearly all answers were correct. Amazing!

Stars are saved only in your current browser. No account or login is required to earn or keep stars.

Privacy Information

Privacy & Data Safety

The game does not request a name, email address, account, or personal profile of any kind. Progress is stored only in the browser using localStorage. No data is sent to any server. This tool is safe for children.
